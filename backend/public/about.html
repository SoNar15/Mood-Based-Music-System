About MOODYYY

An AI-powered music recommendation system that understands your emotions through computer vision.

What is MOODYYY?

Moodyyy is a mood-based music recommendation system that tunes into your emotions to enhance your listening experience. Using computer vision and a custom-trained CNN model, it analyzes facial expressions captured by an ESP32-CAM module and detects emotions in real-time. Based on your mood — whether you're happy, sad, angry, or anything in between — Moodyyy suggests curated songs tailored to how you feel. The system combines a smooth HTML and Tailwind CSS frontend with a robust Node.js and Express.js backend, seamlessly connecting emotion detection with personalized music recommendations. Moodyyy bridges human emotions and technology, creating a more personal and empathetic way to experience music.

Just sit back, relax, and let the music flow as MOODYYY delivers a tailored listening experience.

How Does It Work?

MOODYYY uses advanced machine learning models to analyze facial expressions captured through the ESP32-CAM device. The system processes these images in real-time to identify emotions such as happiness, sadness, anger, and more.

Based on the detected emotion, MOODYYY selects the perfect song from a pre-curated music library and plays it to enhance your mood.

Future Scope

In the future, we plan to integrate more features such as personalized playlists based on users' emotional trends, real-time mood tracking over time, and even integration with smart home devices to adjust the environment based on the detected mood.

We aim to make MOODYYY even more intelligent, personalized, and versatile, bringing the perfect soundtrack to every moment of your life.
